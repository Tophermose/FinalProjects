How We Started

A buddy and I worked at a zoo. The zoo went bankrupt. The manager of the zoo wanted to donate the animals to other zoos that could afford to house them.

My buddy and I tried to convince the manager to sell the animals instead, but she vehemently disagreed. Then she accidentally fell into the tiger enclosure.

We decided to take it upon ourselves to find places for the animals out of the goodness of our hearts. Then my buddy fell in the tiger enclosure too. Such a shame.

Now it is up to me to find housing for these animals.
